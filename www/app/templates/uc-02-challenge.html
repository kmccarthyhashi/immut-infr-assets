'Mutable' vs. 'Immutable'?

Immutable Mutable
= unique and each server modified in-place. Servers updated individually rather than all at once Pros: Provisioning workflows can vary depending on the target Cloud platforms, VMware or Kubernetes environments. Different workflow processes across multiple target environments introduce significant overhead for development, release and maintenance.
Asset sprawl: Image diversity across multiple platforms requires control to prevent loss and unintended exposure. Multiple teams need to manually maintain a ledger of assets in the development, release, and maintenance stages. There is little control over assets' lifecycle, leading to forgetfulness, misplacement, and abandonment.
